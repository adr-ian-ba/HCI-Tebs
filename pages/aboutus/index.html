About Us

Welcome to VKellogg's

Welcome to VKellogg's! Founded in 1906 by Will Keith Kellogg, VKellogg's has grown into a renowned multinational company celebrated for its innovative breakfast foods and snacks. Headquartered in Battle Creek, Michigan, USA, we take pride in being one of the leading cereal manufacturers globally. Our rich history is marked by a commitment to quality and a passion for food product innovation, ensuring that our products bring joy and nourishment to millions of households around the world

As we look towards the future, VKellogg's is excited to enhance our customer service and expand our global reach. We are in the process of designing a user-friendly website to showcase our diverse product range and delicious recipes. Our goal is to create an engaging online platform that aligns with our brand's image and meets the needs of our valued customers

Thank you for being a part of the VKellogg's family. We look forward to continuing to bring you the best in breakfast foods and snacks
